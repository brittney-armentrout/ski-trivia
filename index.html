Ski Resort
Trivia Challenge

This trivia game tests your knowledge of ski areas using real photographs. Click Start to display a ski area photograph. Select the correct resort and click Next to see the next photo. Think quick - you only have 30 seconds for each question! If you run out of time without selecting an answer, the question will be scored as incorrect & the next question will generate. Are you really a beginner, intermediate, or expert on the slopes?! Your score is revealed at the end of the game!
